About SoftCo

SoftCo builds practical information tools for urban residents. Our AQI India Live project was created to provide reliable, easy-to-understand air quality information for Indian cities — combining real-time measurements with clear localised explanations and advice.

Our mission

We aim to help people reduce harmful exposure to air pollution by making air quality data accessible and actionable. We publish original articles, local trends, and everyday tips for families, commuters, and schools.

Editorial approach and sources

Our articles are written by SoftCo's content team and reviewed for accuracy. Live AQI data is fetched from public APIs and official monitoring networks when available — we combine those readings with our original reporting and analysis.
